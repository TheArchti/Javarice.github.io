The situation in Sitio Bebe

Proposals

Now that we've discussed the plans of production, let us offer and present our own solutions that will be further discussed and elaborated on in our educational content. After collecting our funds after the concert, we will first distribute 20% of the funds for air treatment and health treatment. We also plan on providing programs that w ill teach the people of Sitio Bebe to reduce emissions from other sources like burning wood or trash and construction dust. We will start a plantain program to help filter the air pollution. In addition, we would give the people of Sitio Bebe lots of N95 face masks for those who are working in a mining space. Then, we will distribute 40% of our funds to the medical health programs since Sitio Bebe lacks health programs. Next, we will be donating medical equipment such as first aid kits, medicine, vaccines, and nebulizers for those who have respiratory problems. Furthermore, we will use 40% of our funds to install a proper clean water system like water tanks so the community will have constant access to clean water. Lastly, if in case our funds are not enough for a decent water system, we will change the distribution of the funds to 60% and 40% air treatment and medical programs.
